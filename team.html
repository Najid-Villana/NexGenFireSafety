Muhammad Najid

Founder & CEO

Muhammad Najid is a final-year Computer Systems Engineering student at FAST National University, specializing in embedded systems, robotics, AI, and IoT. He is the inventor of the Intelligent Fire Suppression System (IFSS). His work integrates real-time AI vision, automated suppression, and IoT-based alerting to address critical infrastructure safety challenges.

Najid previously interned with the Pakistan Civil Aviation Authority (PCAA), where he worked on generator panels and switchgear systems. His passion lies in applying intelligent automation to life-saving technologies, and he is actively commercializing IFSS through NexGenFireSafety.
